ABOUT

Saint Gallen Cricket Club(SGCC) . Found in 2014 and became the most famous club around the switzerland cricket league in short period as it provided top quality cricket and won titles for itself along with reputation from cricket community around europe. Sgcc won many cricketing fans around switzerland with their performance.
